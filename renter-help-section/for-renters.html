Everything you need to know about renting on Renter

Getting Started

Creating Your Account

  1. Download the Renter app or visit renter.net
  2. Tap/click "Sign Up" or "إنشاء حساب"
  3. Enter your mobile number to receive OTP
  4. Complete your profile with name and email
  5. Verify your identity (required for certain rentals)

Identity Verification

Some items require verified accounts. Verification includes:

  • National ID or Iqama upload
  • Selfie verification
  • Phone number confirmation
ⓘ
Verification typically takes 1-24 hours. Ensure your documents are clear and not expired.

Browsing Items

Find what you need:

  • Browse by category: Cars, Properties, Equipment, Events, Fashion, Services
  • Use search with filters for location, price range, dates
  • Save favorites by tapping the heart icon
  • View owner ratings and reviews before booking

Understanding Listings

Each listing shows:

  • Item photos and description
  • Daily/hourly/weekly pricing
  • Security deposit amount (if applicable)
  • Owner profile, rating, and response time
  • Pickup location or delivery options
  • Cancellation policy
  • Reviews from previous renters

Making a Booking

  1. Select your rental dates/times
  2. Review total cost including any fees
  3. Add any message to the owner (optional)
  4. Select payment method
  5. Confirm booking request
  6. Wait for owner approval (instant or up to 24 hours)

Payment Methods

Accepted payment options:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Mada)
  • Apple Pay
  • STC Pay
  • Renter Wallet (preloaded balance)
🔒
All payments are secured through our payment gateway. Funds are held until rental completion.

Communication with Owners

  • Use in-app chat for all communications
  • Messages are saved for your protection
  • Coordinate pickup/delivery times through chat
  • Report any issues immediately through chat

During Your Rental

  • Take photos/videos at pickup to document condition
  • Report any pre-existing damage immediately
  • Use items according to agreed terms
  • Contact support if issues arise: support@renter.net

Returning Items

  1. Return on agreed date and time
  2. Return in same condition as received
  3. Complete return confirmation with owner
  4. Take photos at return for your records
  5. Security deposit released after inspection

How to Cancel

  1. Go to "My Rentals" > select the booking
  2. Tap "Cancel Booking"
  3. Select cancellation reason
  4. Confirm cancellation
  5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute with an owner:

  1. First, try to resolve directly through in-app chat
  2. If unresolved, open a support ticket within 48 hours of rental end
  3. Provide photos, screenshots, and documentation
  4. Our team will review and mediate
  5. Resolution typically within 3-5 business days

Ratings & Reviews

Rating System

After each rental, you can rate:

  • Overall experience (1-5 stars)
  • Item accuracy (matched description)
  • Communication (owner responsiveness)
  • Item condition

Writing Good Reviews

Help the community with useful reviews:

  • Be specific about what you liked or didn't like
  • Mention if item matched the photos/description
  • Note the owner's communication and flexibility
  • Keep reviews respectful and factual

Account & Security

Profile Management

  • Update personal information in Settings > Profile
  • Add/update payment methods in Settings > Payment
  • Manage notification preferences
  • View and download your rental history

Security Best Practices

  • Never share login credentials
  • Use strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Keep all transactions within the platform
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
⚠
Never pay outside the Renter platform. All legitimate transactions are processed securely through our payment system.

Cars & Vehicles

  • Requirements: Valid Saudi driving license, minimum age 21
  • Verify insurance coverage before pickup
  • Check mileage limits and fuel policy
  • Document car condition thoroughly at pickup
  • Return with same fuel level as received

Properties

Short-Term Rentals (Daily/Weekly)

  • Popular for events, tourism, temporary stays
  • Include amenities list (WiFi, parking, kitchen)
  • Specify check-in/check-out times
  • House rules clearly stated

Long-Term Rentals (Monthly+)

  • Utilities included or separate billing
  • Security deposit typically 1-2 months rent
  • Lease agreement through platform
  • Maintenance responsibility clearly defined

Equipment & Tools

Available categories:

  • Construction equipment (generators, compressors)
  • Photography/Video gear (cameras, lighting)
  • Audio equipment (speakers, microphones)
  • Garden/Outdoor tools
  • Sports equipment
ⓘ
Some equipment may require certifications to operate. Check listing details for any requirements.

Events & Entertainment

Items available for event rentals:

  • Tables, chairs, and linens
  • Tents and canopies
  • Decorations and lighting
  • Catering equipment
  • Entertainment (games, inflatables)
📅
Book early for weekends and holidays. Confirm delivery/setup arrangements in advance.

Fashion & Clothing

Popular rental categories:

  • Wedding dresses and formal wear
  • Traditional/cultural attire
  • Designer bags and accessories
  • Costumes for events

Guidelines:

  • Size charts must be accurate
  • Items cleaned before and after rental
  • Note any alterations policy
  • Damage/stain policy clearly communicated
