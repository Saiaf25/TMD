Everything you need to know about listing and renting your items on Renter

Getting Started

Requirements to List

  • Verified account with ID confirmation
  • Active bank account for receiving payments
  • Valid contact information
  • For vehicles: Valid registration and insurance
  • For properties: Proof of ownership or authorization to sublet

Verification Process

  1. Submit National ID or Iqama
  2. Complete selfie verification
  3. Add bank account details (IBAN)
  4. Review and accept Terms of Service
  5. Approval typically within 24-48 hours

Creating & Managing Listings

Creating Your First Listing

  1. Tap "List Item" or "+ Add Product"
  2. Select category (Cars, Properties, Equipment, etc.)
  3. Add high-quality photos (minimum 3, recommended 8+)
  4. Write detailed title and description
  5. Set pricing (daily, weekly, monthly options)
  6. Set availability calendar
  7. Choose cancellation policy
  8. Set security deposit (if applicable)
  9. Submit for review

Photography Tips

  • Use natural lighting when possible
  • Show multiple angles of the item
  • Include close-ups of important features
  • Show any wear or imperfections honestly
  • For vehicles: exterior, interior, dashboard, trunk
  • For properties: all rooms, amenities, view

Pricing Strategies

  • Research similar listings in your area
  • Offer weekly/monthly discounts (10-20% off)
  • Consider seasonal pricing adjustments
  • Start competitive to build reviews

Optimizing Your Listing

  • Use keywords lessees search for
  • Update calendar regularly
  • Respond to inquiries quickly (within 1 hour ideal)
  • Keep photos current and accurate
  • Encourage reviews from satisfied lessees
💡
Listings with 8+ photos and detailed descriptions get up to 3x more bookings than minimal listings.

Managing Bookings

Handling Booking Requests

When you receive a booking request:

  1. Review lessee's profile and ratings
  2. Check your availability for requested dates
  3. Respond within 24 hours (faster = better ranking)
  4. Accept, decline, or send a message

Calendar Management

  • Block dates when item is unavailable
  • Set minimum/maximum rental duration
  • Add buffer time between rentals for preparation
  • Sync with external calendars if needed

Handover Process

At pickup:

  1. Verify lessee's identity
  2. Document item condition with photos/video together
  3. Explain operation/usage if needed
  4. Confirm rental dates and return expectations
  5. Complete in-app handover confirmation
⚠
Always document the item condition with photos/video at handover. This is your protection in case of damage disputes.

Payments & Earnings

Payment Timeline

How and when you get paid:

  1. Lessee pays at booking confirmation
  2. Funds held securely by Renter
  3. Payment released 24 hours after successful return
  4. Transfer to your bank within 2-3 business days

Earnings Dashboard

Track your performance:

  • Total earnings (monthly, yearly)
  • Pending payments
  • Completed payouts
  • Download statements for accounting
ⓘ
SAREI (fast payment service) has a daily blackout from 2:00 PM - 8:00 AM Saudi time, and is unavailable on weekends and holidays.

Protecting Your Items

Security Deposits

Setting appropriate deposits:

  • Set based on item value and risk
  • Deposit held by platform during rental
  • Released to lessee after successful return
  • Claim against deposit within 48 hours of return

Insurance Coverage

Protection options:

  • Basic protection included for verified rentals
  • Enhanced coverage available for high-value items
  • Vehicle rentals require valid insurance
  • Document everything for claims

Handling Damage

  1. Document damage with photos/video immediately
  2. Report through app within 48 hours
  3. Get repair estimates if needed
  4. Submit claim with all documentation
  5. Resolution within 5-7 business days
