🛡 Protection Policies For lessees & lessors

Safety Tips for Lessees

  • Always verify lessor identity at pickup
  • Meet in public places when possible
  • Document everything with photos and video
  • Keep all communication within the app
  • Never pay outside the platform
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, cancel
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately

Safety Tips for Lessors

  • Verify lessee identity before handover
  • Set appropriate security deposits
  • Document item condition thoroughly
  • Review lessee ratings before accepting
  • Use GPS tracking for valuable items (vehicles)
  • Maintain proper insurance coverage
  • Never share personal banking details directly

Recognizing Scams

Be aware of these warning signs:

⚠ Warning Signs to Watch For
  • Requests to pay outside the platform
  • Prices that seem too good to be true
  • Pressure to make quick decisions
  • Requests for personal/financial information
  • Poor quality or stock photos
  • New accounts with no reviews
  • Communication moving outside the app
