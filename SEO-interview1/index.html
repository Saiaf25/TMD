3

Technical SEO Deep-Dive

10 min
  • Scenario — Renting Marketplace

    We have a renting marketplace with thousands of listing pages across Saudi cities. Many pages are thin or near-duplicate (same template, different city/district). Crawl budget is being wasted. How do you approach this?

    Looking for: canonicalization, noindex strategy, programmatic SEO understanding, faceted navigation handling
  • Scenario — Real Estate Site

    The real estate site has 200+ property pages but Google is only indexing 60. What's your diagnosis process?

    Looking for: GSC coverage report, crawl analysis, thin content audit, internal linking, sitemap review
  • Schema: Beyond basic FAQ/Article — have you implemented RealEstate, Product, or LocalBusiness schema? How would you approach schema for rental listings?
  • Core Web Vitals: A client's LCP is 5.2s on mobile. Walk me through diagnosis.
4

Content Strategy — Project-Specific

10 min
  • Scenario — SaaS App

    Our SaaS product targets Saudi businesses. We need to build organic traffic that generates leads, not just visits. How would you structure the content strategy? What does the funnel look like?

    Looking for: TOFU/MOFU/BOFU thinking, content hubs, pillar pages, lead magnets, CTA strategy
  • Arabic SEO: How do you handle Arabic keyword research for Saudi specifically? (MSA vs. Saudi dialect, search volume tools for Arabic, local intent)
  • Renting marketplace content: Beyond listings, what content would you create to build topical authority for a rental platform in Saudi? Looking for: city guides, renting guides, neighborhood comparisons, legal/regulatory content
  • How do you measure content ROI — not just traffic, but actual leads/conversions? What does your reporting look like?
5

Working Model & Logistics

10 min
  • I need to delegate SEO responsibility, not micromanage. Can you work independently? What does that look like in practice?
  • What would your monthly deliverable/report look like for each project?
  • What tools do you need access to? (Ahrefs, Semrush, etc. — license costs matter)
  • Have you managed freelance/part-time arrangements before while holding a full-time role? How did you handle conflicts?
  • Rate: What do you charge — hourly, monthly retainer, or per-project? Get a clear number. Clarify if it covers all 3 projects or per-project.
  • Capacity check: Can you realistically handle 3 Saudi projects alongside your full-time role? Be direct. Overcommitment is the #1 risk with freelancers who have day jobs.
  • When can you start? What does onboarding look like for you?
6

Quick-Fire — Mindset & Depth

5 min
  • What's one SEO project that failed or underperformed? What happened and what did you learn?
  • How do you stay updated on algorithm changes and new SEO developments?
  • What's your take on AI-generated content for SEO in 2026?
  • How are AI Overviews / GEO changing your approach to SEO?
  • If you could only pick one metric to judge SEO success, what would it be and why?

Watch Out For

  • Buzzword overload with no specifics — push for real numbers, real URLs, real screenshots
  • Overcommitment — full-time + 3 freelance projects is a lot. Press on time management
  • No portfolio or examples after you explicitly asked for one
  • Agency mindset (surface-level, volume-based) vs. depth needed for 3 owned projects
  • No experience with marketplace/programmatic SEO — the renting site needs this
  • Vague on Saudi market specifics — says "Arabic SEO" but can't differentiate Saudi from UAE
  • Can't articulate a failure or learning moment — signals lack of self-awareness
